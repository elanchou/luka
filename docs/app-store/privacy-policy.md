# Sault. Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 8, 2026

Sault. is a local-first vault app for storing seed phrases, private keys, and secure notes on your device.

## What Sault. does

- Encrypts vault data locally on your device
- Stores encrypted vault records in local app storage
- Uses your master password to derive an encryption key
- Optionally allows you to back up vault data to your own iCloud account
- Lets you export vault data only when you explicitly choose to do so

## Data collection

Sault. does not run a user account system and does not collect personal data for analytics, advertising, or profiling.

Sault. does not:

- sell personal data
- track users across apps or websites
- use third-party advertising SDKs
- upload vault contents to developer-operated servers

## Data stored on device

Vault content such as seed phrases, private keys, secure notes, and activity history is stored locally on your device in encrypted form.

Your master password is used to derive an encryption key. Sault. does not transmit your master password to the developer.

## iCloud backup

If you enable iCloud backup, encrypted vault files and related key metadata may be stored in your personal iCloud container so you can restore your vault on your own Apple devices.

This backup is controlled by your Apple account. Sault. does not use a developer-hosted backend to store your vault.

## Photo library access

Sault. may request photo library permission only when you explicitly choose a flow that saves or shares exported files or images. This access is user-initiated and is not used for tracking.

## Security

Sault. is designed to keep sensitive data local and encrypted. However, no software can guarantee absolute security. You are responsible for:

- choosing a strong master password
- protecting your device
- handling exported files carefully
