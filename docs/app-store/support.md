# Sault. Support

Sault. is a local-first secure vault for seed phrases, private keys, and sensitive notes.

## Getting help

- Support email: lucidity95@gmail.com
- GitHub issues: https://github.com/elanchou/luka/issues

## Common topics

### I forgot my master password

Sault. cannot recover your master password. Your password is used to derive the local encryption key, so please store it safely.

### Where is my data stored

Vault data is stored locally on your device in encrypted form. If you enable iCloud backup, encrypted backup files may also be stored in your personal iCloud container.

### Can I export my data

Yes. Sault. includes an export flow for user-initiated backup or transfer. Exported files should be handled carefully because they may not remain protected once shared outside the app.

### Does Sault. require an online account

No. Sault. does not require account registration or a developer-hosted login flow.

## Response expectations

Support requests are handled on a best-effort basis. Please include:

- iOS version

- a short reproduction description
