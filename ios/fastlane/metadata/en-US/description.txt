Sault. is a private, local-first vault for storing seed phrases, private keys, and sensitive notes on iPhone and iPad.

Built for users who want calm, premium security without cloud account friction, Sault. keeps your most sensitive records encrypted on device and protected by a master password.

Key features:
- Local encrypted vault for seed phrases, private keys, and secure notes
- Master password protection with PBKDF2-based key derivation
- AES-encrypted local storage for sensitive content
- Optional biometric unlock support with Face ID or Touch ID
- Search and filter for faster retrieval
- Activity log for important vault actions
- Optional encrypted iCloud backup and restore flow
- Manual export flow when you explicitly choose to share or back up your data

Sault. is designed for privacy-conscious users who prefer local control, clean design, and deliberate security workflows.

Important:
- Keep your master password safe. It cannot be recovered by the developer.
- Handle exported files carefully.
- iCloud backup is optional and uses your own Apple account.
